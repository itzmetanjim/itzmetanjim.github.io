Selected Works

Three-Body Simulation

A comprehensive visualization of the three-body problem, one of the most fascinating unsolved problems in classical mechanics. Each point in this 2D plot corresponds to a particle, with colors indicating which of three massive attractors the particle eventually collides with. The resulting patterns reveal the chaotic, yet beautiful nature of gravitational dynamics.

Double Pendulum Divergence Map

Double pendulums are deceptively simple systems that exhibit profoundly chaotic behavior. This visualization maps initial conditions to their eventual divergence, with brightness indicating the degree of chaos. The result reveals unexpected islands of stability amidst seas of unpredictability—a visual proof of sensitive dependence on initial conditions.
